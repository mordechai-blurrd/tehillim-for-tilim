How It Works
Simple. Fast. Global.
📡 Where do alerts come from?
We monitor the official Pikud Ha'oref (Israel Home Front Command) API in real time — the same system powering Red Alert apps inside Israel. A fallback to tzevaadom.co.il ensures near-zero downtime.
🌍 Does it work outside Israel?
Yes — that's the entire point. Email and WhatsApp work everywhere. SMS uses Twilio, covering 180+ countries. Whether you're in New York, London, Melbourne, or Tel Aviv, you'll know the moment the sirens sound.
💬 How does WhatsApp work?
We use the Twilio WhatsApp API. You need to activate once — tap here to send the activation message. Alerts will then arrive directly to your WhatsApp.
📖 What should I say?
Any Tehillim you know. Chapter 121 — שיר למעלות — is especially powerful. Even a single verse said with full intention rises straight to Heaven. Stop, take a breath, and connect.
🔕 How often will I get alerts?
Only during real rocket alert events. Rapid bursts of sirens within a short window are grouped into one notification so you're not overwhelmed.
