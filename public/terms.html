1. What This Service Does

Tehillim for Tilim is a free notification service that alerts subscribers when rockets are fired toward Israel, based on data from Pikud Ha'oref (Israel Home Front Command) and other public sources. The purpose of the service is to give Jews around the world the opportunity to stop and say Tehillim in real time for those in danger.

Notifications are delivered via email, WhatsApp, SMS, and browser push notifications, depending on your chosen preferences.
